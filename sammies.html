Sammies — Collapsible Outdoor Shoe

A collapsible, waterproof, lightweight shoe engineered for explorers who refuse to compromise on comfort, performance, or portability.

Problem

Outdoor shoes are traditionally rigid, bulky, and inefficient to pack.

Backpackers and outdoor explorers need a warm, lightweight, comfortable shoe for camp—without adding significant weight or losing performance.

Conventional footwear makes this nearly impossible due to stiff materials and non-collapsible constructions.

Solution

Sammies deliver a completely new approach to outdoor footwear:

A waterproof, collapsible shoe made from high-performance materials and an engineered folding sole. It packs flat, adapts to different terrains, and provides warmth, portability, and comfort in a single lightweight system.

Key aspects of the solution:

  • Collapsible sole geometry engineered for compactness
  • Waterproof outer body
  • Synthetic down insulation
  • Lightweight TPU bottom
  • Comfortable, adaptable fit

Design

The design of Sammies blends performance materials with collapsible engineering:

  • Nylon Upper: Lightweight, water-resistant, durable
  • TPU Sole: Flexible but structured for outdoor use
  • Synthetic Down Interior: Warmth without weight
  • Collapsible Geometry: Sole channels compress and fold
  • Ergonomic Fit: Designed around natural foot motion
  • Packability: Folds flat to minimize backpack space

The design goal was simple:

A shoe that performs like outdoor gear, but packs like clothing.

Fabrication

Fabrication involved multiple phases:

  • CAD Modeling: Full geometry created in SolidWorks
  • Material Sourcing: Outdoor-grade, waterproof nylon & TPU sole
  • Prototype Stitching: Early uppers hand-assembled to test fit
  • Sole Prototypes: Multiple TPU sole versions tested
  • Compression Testing: Ensuring the sole collapses without losing durability
  • Assembly Process: Combined upper, insulation layer, and sole bonding

The fabrication process prioritized durability, flexible geometry, and water resistance.

Iterations

Sammies went through numerous iteration cycles:

  • Adjusted outsole geometry for optimal folding
  • Improved wall thickness to balance flexibility & strength
  • Modified insulation levels for warmth vs. packability
  • Multiple comfort & fit adjustments
  • Sole compression resistance testing
  • Ease-of-fold redesign to improve packability

Each prototype moved closer toward the final balance of:

  • comfort
  • durability
  • warmth
  • flexibility
  • compactness

Lessons

Key lessons from the development of Sammies:

  • Flexibility vs durability is a delicate balance
  • Material layering impacts collapse performance
  • TPU thickness and geometry dramatically affect pack-down behavior
  • Precision manufacturing tolerances are critical in footwear
  • Outdoor shoes can be reimagined when traditional rigidity is removed

Sammies represent an evolved, user-centered approach to footwear for explorers who value comfort and packability.
